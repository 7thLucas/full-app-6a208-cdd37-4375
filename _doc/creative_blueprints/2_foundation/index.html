The Foundation

Sending a package shouldn't feel like a leap of faith.

The Problem

Custody goes dark the moment it leaves your hands.

  • • No idea where the package actually is.
  • • "Was it delivered?" — and to whom?
  • • No proof of condition at pickup or drop-off.
  • • Disputes become one person's word against another's.
The Solution

A live trust layer over every delivery.

  • ✓ Watch the courier move on a live map.
  • ✓ Photo proof captured at pickup and drop-off.
  • ✓ E-signature confirms the recipient got it.
  • ✓ Fast matching keeps couriers earning, customers confident.

Pakettt! connects customers and couriers in a marketplace where every handoff is visible and verifiable — not just "request a courier," but delivery you can prove.
