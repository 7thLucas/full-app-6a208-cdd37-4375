# Pakettt! — Product

## What it is
Pakettt! is a package-delivery marketplace MVP connecting customers who want to send packages with couriers who carry them. The core promise is a **live trust layer**: every handoff is visible and verifiable.

## Differentiator (lead of every pitch)
- Real-time map tracking — customers always know where their package is.
- Photo proof at both pickup and drop-off — condition and custody documented.
- E-signature on receipt — confirms the recipient actually got it.
- Fast courier matching — keeps couriers earning and customers confident.

Pakettt! is not just "request a courier." It is verifiable delivery you can watch happen.

## Users / Roles
- **Customer**: register/login; create delivery requests (pickup & drop-off addresses, package size/weight/category/special instructions); see estimated cost; track status in real time; chat with the assigned courier; view delivery history; rate completed deliveries.
- **Courier**: register/login; complete profile & vehicle verification; toggle online/offline; receive/accept/reject jobs; navigate to pickup & drop-off; update status through the journey; upload photo proof of pickup & delivery + capture e-signature; chat with customers; view earnings & delivery history.
- **Admin**: manage customers & couriers; verify courier accounts; monitor active deliveries; manage delivery pricing rules; review disputes; access operational analytics; oversee the network.

## Core delivery workflow
1. Customer creates a delivery request.
2. Nearby available couriers receive it.
3. A courier accepts the job.
4. Courier travels to pickup.
5. Courier confirms pickup with photo proof.
6. Customer tracks courier location live.
7. Courier delivers the package.
8. Recipient signs / confirms receipt.
9. Courier uploads proof of delivery.
10. Delivery marked completed; both parties rate.

## Delivery statuses
Pending → Searching for Courier → Courier Assigned → Courier En Route to Pickup → Package Picked Up → In Transit → Arriving Soon → Delivered. Cancelled is a terminal exit available before completion.

## Key features (all in MVP)
Auth + role-based access control; real-time GPS tracking; interactive maps; delivery request & package info management; status tracking with timeline; push notifications; in-app chat (customer ↔ courier); photo proof of pickup & delivery; e-signature capture; delivery history; ratings & reviews; demo/seed data; cloud file storage for proof photos; secure auth.

## Brand & tone
Clean, modern, logistics-focused. Calm, confident, trustworthy. Fast and frictionless, never busy. Mobile-first for both customers and couriers.

## MVP scope boundary
Out of scope (do NOT build): digital wallets, multi-stop deliveries, business accounts, subscriptions, international shipping.

## UI/UX principles
Fast booking with minimal steps. A clear tracking timeline. A live map tracking screen. Mobile-friendly for both customers and couriers.
